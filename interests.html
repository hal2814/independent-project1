Interests/hobbies/skills

Hobbies: I like to make eletronic music and recorded music in my spare time with Ableton live. I find new and unique video games to be fun. I'll take my dogs to the dog park in my free time and like to work on training them and teaching them new tricks.

Skills: I can professionally record musical artists. I have recorded a wide range of genres from small bands to large orchestras. I'm able to cook pretty well without recipes and have a great understanding of flavor profiles. I can produce my own music and play instruments such as drums, guitar, bass, keys, and even trumpet.
